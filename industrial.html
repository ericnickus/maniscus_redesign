OWNERS

Meniscus Group offers individual property owners a personal and flexible approach to free up real estate capital to reinvest in growing their business.

Entrepreneurs at heart, the Meniscus Group principals understand the commitment and work it takes to grow any business, and take a personalized approach on sale leaseback or exit options that work best for business owners.

For its tenants, Meniscus Group provides predictability, accessibility and operational benefits from routine building maintenance to large, custom build-outs. As a long-term asset holder, Meniscus Group values tenant relationships and maintains a long-term vision over short-term gain.
