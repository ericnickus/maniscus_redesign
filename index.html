INVESTMENT CRITERIA

MENISCUS GROUP IS AN INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT GROUP LOOKING TO EXPAND ITS PORTFOLIO OF PROPERTIES WITH A DIVERSE MIX OF BUILDING CLASSES, BUSINESS AND TENANTS.

  • Industrial Service Facilities with Low Coverage and Significant Outdoor Storage, Bulk Distribution and Light Manufacturing
  • Building Size Range: Up to 500,000 sq ft
  • Site Coverage Range: 5%-50%
  • Deal Cost Range: $5 million-$100 million
  • Building Asset Class: A to C, Stabilized and Vacant
  • Tenant Size: 20,000 sq ft or more
  • Location Preferences: In major US and emerging markets with interstate access, intermodal, proximity to airport and/or port locations
  • Structure Types: Concrete Tilt Wall, Metal, Block, and Parking Facilities
  • Transaction Types: Single Asset and Portfolio Transactions, Single and Multi-Tenant Assets, Short-term or Long-term Leases and Sale/Leasebacks and Vacant
  • Speculative Investments: Complex properties including buildings that are in need of major renovation, demolition, and/or repurposing.
